As Bear Bile Farms Close, Captive Bears are at Risk

date

by: Annie Roth




As the bear bile industry crashes, the lives of nearly 1,000 bears in captive are at risk. As farmers and producers close down their farms, some have admitted to killing the bears. In order to have another source of income from the bears, they are killed for others body parts like their paws, teeths, claws, and gallbladders. They are sold in the black market for very high prices.
